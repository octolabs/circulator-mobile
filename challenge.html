Overview
For over a year the District of Columbia has provided public access to real-time transit data related to DC Circulator through the Internet. Use the data catalog below to access data in XML, Text/CSV, KML or ESRI Shapefile formats. Please note that by accessing the data catalog, you agree to our Terms of Use. Please read before accessing the data. If you have any questions or comments about the data, please contact us at octolabs@dc.gov or even better join our Google Group. Visit DC FOIA page for information on FOIA requests.
